Meet Cristina

Cristina is an adventurer at heart. For example, she loves to travel. Whenever she gets the chance she loves to escape to exotic places where she enjoys meeting new people and learning about their culture. When Cristina is not traveling different countries she often finds herself exploring the ocean as she is an avid scuba diver. She loves the ocean. So being in the Malibu gives her the opportunity to go on hikes where she can get great views from the beautiful ocean-side hilltops.

In addition immersing herself in these exciting activities, Cristina has a passion for living a active and healthy lifestyle. She does this by regularly practicing Yoga and concocting delicious but healthy recipes. Not only does she use these techniqes to enrich her life, but also the lives of others. Cristina also has a passion for sharing her knowledge with people around the world through her own website, revitalizemindbodysoul.
